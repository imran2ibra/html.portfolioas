shumileta

"Shumileta" is a Tanzanian horror film featuring Janety Mwaipaja as Shumileta, Evans Moshi as Mack, and Jennifer Kyaka as Monte. The plot follows Mack's journey to Ujiji, a mystical city of spirits, where the traditional healer Ndele strives to restore Monte to human form. The film delves into themes of witchcraft and the supernatural, depicting Mack's struggle to return to the human world. "Shumileta" has gained popularity beyond Tanzania, notably in the Democratic Republic of Congo, where it is among the most renowned Tanzanian films.

Maria

"Maria" is a Kenyan prime-time television drama series that aired on Citizen TV from October 10, 2019, to March 18, 2021, with 374 episodes. The series was created by Lulu Hassan.

The narrative centers on Maria, portrayed by Yasmin Said, an uneducated orphan from the ghetto. After her mother's death, Maria is adopted by William Hausa, a wealthy man from the city's affluent suburbs. Within the Hausa household, Maria encounters Luwi, the family's last-born son. Despite Luwi's existing marriage to the cunning Sofia, he falls deeply in love with Maria.
